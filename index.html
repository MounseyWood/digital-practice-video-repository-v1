CLO 3D Digital Practice Video Library

Your comprehensive collection of tutorials, insights, and industry expertise to support your creative journey in digital fashion design

Welcome to Your Digital Fashion Library

This curated collection of videos will help you develop the digital competencies essential for contemporary fashion practice. Each tutorial is designed to support your creative and technical development with industry-standard digital tools and techniques.

When you click on any video thumbnail, you'll be redirected to our Microsoft Stream server where you'll find full video transcripts and chapter timestamps to help you navigate to specific topics quickly.

Creating Avatars & Systematic Drafting

Learn to create custom avatars that match your target market's measurements. This comprehensive tutorial covers measuring studio mannequins, creating digital twins, and building avatars for different demographics including plus-size, petite, maternity, and adaptive clothing.

Beginner Level 62 min

Copying Patterns & Source Pieces

Master the art of digitising existing patterns and garments. This tutorial shows you how to trace patterns from books, photograph and import physical patterns, and use CLO's parametric tools to recreate garments from source pieces.

Beginner Level 28 min

Importing Files & Adding Fullness

A comprehensive guide to importing DXF and PDF pattern files into CLO 3D, plus advanced techniques for adding volume and texture to your designs. Learn slash-and-spread methods, create gathering and smocking effects, add ruffles and pleats.

Beginner Level 1 hr 49 min

Digital Fabrics Fundamentals

Understand the science behind digital fabric creation in CLO 3D. Learn about PBR (Physically Based Rendering) workflows, texture mapping, and how different material properties affect the look and feel of your virtual garments.

Beginner Level 41 min

Advanced Digital Fabric Creation

Take your fabric knowledge further with advanced material properties and custom texture creation. Learn to work with metallic, velvet, fur, and iridescent materials, understand roughness and reflection mapping, and create your own fabric textures from photographs.

Intermediate Level 40 min

Modular Configurator & Pen Tools

Explore CLO's modular configurator for rapid prototyping and the powerful pen tool for creating fitted garments. Learn to mix and match pre-built pattern components, draw directly onto avatars for sportswear and activewear, and create professional sweatshirts.

Beginner Level 1 hr 16 min

Preparing Assets for Production

Take your digital designs to production with this comprehensive guide to pattern grading, lay planning, and working with modern microfactories. Learn to add seam allowances, create size runs, calculate fabric requirements, and prepare files for digital printing.

Intermediate Level 1 hr 13 min

Digital Pipeline: CLO 3D to DAZ 3D

Discover how to create photorealistic fashion imagery by connecting CLO 3D with DAZ 3D. Learn to export your digital garments, create stunning avatars, and produce professional-quality renders for marketing, portfolios, and e-commerce.

Intermediate Level 36 min
